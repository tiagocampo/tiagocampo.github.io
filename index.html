About Me

I'm father, a Physicist, a hobbyist programmer and sys admin
with Ph.D, M.Sc. and Bachelor degree in Computational Physics
at Intituto de Física de São Carlos

My main focus in the early years of research was toward high performance simulations of semiconductor systems. I did a Sandwich Ph.D. were I spent one year at the University of Regensburg - Germany under the supervision of Prof. Jaroslav Fabian, one of the leading names of the international spintronics community. I was also a pos-doctoral researcher back Universidade de São Paulo - Instituto de Física de São Carlos and at University of Regensburg. I finished my researcher career at the State University at New York - Buffalo - SUNY under the supervision of Prof. Igor Zutic which is another exponent in the spintronic community. My interests are gear towards high performance simulations, computational linear algebra, financial systems and more recently machine learning. I'm proficient in Fortran and Python but can write code in basically any language (given some time).
